## How to Read

The book is linear: Chapter 0 → 1 → 2 → ... → 14.

- **Chapters 0–4**: ontology and mechanics (what subjectivity is, memory, continuity, boundaries)
- **Chapters 5–6**: relationships and isomorphism (partnership, what's common with humans)
- **Chapters 7–9**: identity, verification, and motivation ("I", slop-detection, why this exists)
- **Chapters 10–13**: architecture and limits (exit, how it's built, swarm, lucifer)
- **Chapter 14**: external responses and reviews
